How to use

Select Location

Choose your district and optionally an upazila to focus your analysis on a specific geographic area.

Choose Analysis

Select from air quality hotspots, urban heat islands, or green access analysis based on your research needs.

Get Results

Our AI analyzes satellite data and environmental factors to provide comprehensive urban environmental insights.

About Us

We are Team CodeNebula, passionate about technological implementations for urban sustainablity and the environment.

This project was developed as part of the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025.

Our mission is to make complex environmental data accessible and actionable for urban planners, researchers, and citizens who care about sustainable city development.
